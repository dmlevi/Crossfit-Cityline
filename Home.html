The Truth

How did we end up here? At our computers, typing furiously, bouncing from emails to websites to smart phones and back. There’s tons of movement…but only with our eyes. In this age of technology, our bodies have taken a back seat. We neglect them, depriving them of the activities they were build to do. It’s the truth. We’ve all been there.

The Opportunity

At CrossFit City Line we are far removed from the days of walking around, obsessing and contemplating about what to do in the gym today, putting on a hoodie and headphones and sporting a big ego. Where that happens is where fitness goals go to die. They fall by the wayside, never get achieved and end up forgotten until the ball drops at years end and a new year begins. Fitness cannot wait. Life cannot wait. We are here. Now. Goals need to be set and then achieved. This is your call to action.

The Challenge

CrossFit may look intimidating and chaotic at first. But after learning the philosophy and training methods behind the program and understanding and seeing how CrossFit works with us at CrossFit City Line, it becomes clear that it is more than just a workout. We help you create a healthy lifestyle: nutritional choices, a state of mind, a way of being.

We Empower Results.

About Us

Crossfit

We are a true Crossfit "Box" with an emphasis on community engagement. At CrossFit City Line, we get you RESULTS and you'll have a blast doing it. Our program is right for you and is modified to be appropriate for any ability level. You won’t be asked to lift weights or do any movement beyond your ability. We’re proud to work with people of all ages and abilities will help you achieve your short and long term goals!
